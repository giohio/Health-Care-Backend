Welcome to [ECGpedia](https://ecgpedia.org/wiki/Frequently_Asked_Questions "Frequently Asked Questions"), a free electrocardiography (ECG) tutorial and textbook to which anyone can contribute , designed for medical professionals such as cardiac care nurses and physicians. ECGpedia has received more than 9.300.000 unique visits from 238 countries. Also visit our new [Textbook of Cardiology](http://www.textbookofcardiology.org/wiki/Main_Page).* [ECG course](https://ecgpedia.org/wiki/ECG_course "ECG course") * [ECG textbook](https://ecgpedia.org/wiki/Textbook "Textbook") * [Practice ECGs](https://ecgpedia.org/wiki/Practice_ECGs "Practice ECGs")![Image 1](https://ecgpedia.org/images/thumb/4/49/Jonas_de_Jong.png/75px-Jonas_de_Jong.png)Jonas de Jong, cardiologist at the OLVG, The Netherlands & founder of ECGpedia
